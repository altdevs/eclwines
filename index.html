About Us

Eclectic Wines

Eclectic Wines has been Importing and promoting the wines of Greece for more than a decade.In 2011 we were voted as one of Imbibe Magazines Small Importers of the year.


Along route we have added a couple of non Greek wineries to our portfolio.
